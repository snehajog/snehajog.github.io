Final Project

My final project is two pieces on how distorted using social media is. The first image is looking at repetition and how often people use social media and see unrealistic accounts and posts. The second is exploring how people have grown up with social media, there are old devices like iPods and new ones like an iPad. I combined the screens with a charcoal self portrait I made in an Ani class freshman year. The glitches are to show how distorted it is and how social media is in people's lives a lot more than they think.
